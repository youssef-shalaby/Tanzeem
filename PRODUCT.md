# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Tanzeem serves inventory operators, managers, and business admins who need to keep products, stock movements, suppliers, orders, alerts, and branch-level work organized. Users are often doing repeated operational tasks under time pressure, so the product should favor clarity, predictable navigation, and fast scanning over decorative complexity.

## Product Purpose
Tanzeem helps businesses manage inventory across branches: tracking stock, products, transactions, suppliers, orders, delivery issues, alerts, analytics, employees, roles, and workspace settings. Success means fewer missed stock problems, clearer team accountability, and faster day-to-day inventory decisions.

## Brand Personality
Calm, capable, and orderly. The public pages should feel polished and trustworthy, while the app should feel focused and work-ready.

## Anti-references
Avoid generic SaaS hype, fake social proof, confusing permission behavior, payment screens that imply a real charge without an integrated provider, and decorative layouts that hide the actual inventory product.

## Design Principles
Show the product doing real work.
Make access rules visible and consistent.
Keep operational screens dense enough for repeated use.
Use clear labels over buzzwords.
Treat onboarding, pricing, and payment as trust-building moments.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Aim for WCAG AA contrast, visible focus states, keyboard-reachable navigation, useful alt text, and reduced-motion fallbacks for animated public surfaces.
